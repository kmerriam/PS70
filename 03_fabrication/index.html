<div class="textcontainer"> <p class="margin"> </p> <h3>Week 3: Hand Tools and Fabrication</h3> <h4>Kinetic Sculpture</h4> <br> </br> I made a ballerina because my friend is a ballerina and her birthday is coming up so I thought I could make something for her. I ran out of time so it is a rough piece but it does move! I used copper wire, and pliers to bend the wire. I also used wrapping write to connect some of the pieces but then resorted to hot glue due to time. I used modeling clay to make the pink details and to mount it on to the base. I also used a wire cutter to cut the wires into the desired size. I used the laser cutter to cut out a circle base of out cardboard and then attached the ballerina using the white clay to the base. <video width="640" height="480" controls> <source src="ballerina.mp4" type="video/mp4"> </video> </div>
